# Welcome to John & Jeri's Corner of the Internet

We're John and Jeri — two people who love to travel, cook, and overshare. This little website has been our digital home base since 2006, and we're always finding new reasons (and new tech) to keep it alive.

> 🎨 The pig favicon you see in the browser tab? That's a hand-drawn original by the very talented **Sidney**.

---

## 📖 Our Story

This site has grown up right alongside the web itself.

### 2006 — The Travel Blog Era

What started as a personal tech experiment quickly became a real travel diary. We used it to document a journey through **Australia** and **New Zealand**, with a custom-built comment section so family and friends back home could follow along from across the globe.

[![Welcome to Sydney|500](http://johnandjeri.com/images/carousel/welcometosydney.jpg)](http://johnandjeri.com/album)

### 2007 — The Recipes Move In

The site grew a kitchen. We added a digital cookbook to collect and share family favorites — the kind of recipes that get requested at every holiday. Those recipes are still here today, and there's always room for more.

[![Recipe collection|500](http://johnandjeri.com/images/recipeHeader.jpg)](http://johnandjeri.com/recipe)

### 2026 — The Modern Refresh

Twenty years in, the original codebase got a full architectural overhaul. The legacy app has been retired in favor of a modern, cloud-native stack — rebuilt from scratch to be fast, clean, and a lot more fun to work on.

## 🚀 What's Under the Hood

The current site is built with a stack we're genuinely proud of:

**Backend & Orchestration**
- [**.NET 10**](https://dotnet.microsoft.com/download) — Latest C# runtime with the newest language features and performance improvements
- [**.NET Aspire**](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/dotnet/aspire/get-started/aspire-overview) — Cloud-native orchestration for service discovery, telemetry, and resilience
- [**Google Authentication**](https://developers.google.com/identity) — Secure, token-based sign-in via Google OAuth

**Frontend**
- [**React & TypeScript**](https://react.dev) — Component-driven UI with strong typing throughout
- [**Mantine UI**](https://mantine.dev) — Beautiful, accessible React component library
- [**Vite**](https://vitejs.dev) — Lightning-fast frontend build tooling

**Data & DevOps**
- [**SQL Server**](https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/sql-server) — Reliable relational storage for posts, recipes, and more
- [**GitHub Actions**](https://github.com/features/actions) — Automated CI/CD for builds and deployments to production
